Finpulp.com

AI powered APIs for Finance

We are a startup focussed on finance domain. We work in the area of applying Artifitial Intelligence to various problems in the Finance domain. We provide data intelligence on to top of publicly available data so that Financial Institutions can make intelligent decisions based on that data

What we offer?

We apply intelligent algorithms on top of existing public( or private) data and expose the resulting data, as secure APIs. We also customize the algorithms according to your need.

Use Cases

  • We helped some high volume traders to implement trading strategies based on past asset (stock , Mutual Funds and ETFs) price data and a number of other parameters. This helped them reduce the amount of time they spend on excel sheets to pour over tons of data and instead they were able to execute their strategies. They increased their Stock breadth from 10s of stocks to 800 stocks.
  • We helped wealth managers to profile users and provide assets for investment according to their risk profile.
  • We provide custom algorithms on top of Stock price data to calculate risk, return, Sharpe ratio, Sortino, DMA, Price - Volume break out , Define support and resistances for a asset.
  • We provide algorithms for Portfolio analysis and Portfolio re-balancing using Capital Asset pricing model , Model Portfolio Theory etc.
  • We provide institutions or traders with automated News analysis capabilities.
  • We provide intelligent systems to help Financial Institutions to engage actively with their customers, chat bots, intelligent customer care etc
  • We are in the process of training Artifitial Neural Nets with Stock data to unleash prediction power
